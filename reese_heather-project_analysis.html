Truffles and Trifles Site Redesign

Site Intent

The intent of the redesign is to make the site more user-friendly, and to make the content more readable. The intent is also to make the site more modern, and reflect the interest of the target audience. These goals will be achieved by redesigning the graphics, including the header, and complimentary graphics. We will also be streamlining by adding a footer, having a main content section, easier navigation, and one sidebar.

Competitive Analysis

Gourmet Magic

Gourmet Magic is another Orlando based site that offers culinary classes, and also offers etiquette classes as well. They share common search terms, such as 'cooking classes', and 'cooking classes orlando'. The global Alexa ranking for Gourmet Magic is 20,253,753. They have an estimated 9 daily unique visitors to the site, and a Google Page Rank of 1. The site has been registered for 5 years 7 months. Because of the high Alexa rating, there is no provided analytics for time spent on site, and audience demographics.

Chefs.com

Chefs.com is a site that offers listings for cooking classes, recipes, videos and blogs related to cooking. They are not a local site, but offer listings of cooking courses for locations across the country. The global Alexa ranking for Gourmet Magic is 839,376. They have an estimated 196 daily unique visitors to the site, and a Google Page Rank of 4. The site has been registered for 16 years 4 months. The main demographic is 45-54 year old females with at least some college experience. this demographic is very similar to the demographic that frequents Truffles and Trifles.

Targeted Audience

According to Alexa, the main site demographic is females from the ages of 25-34, with a college degree. A small amount have children, and most are browsing the site from work. However, from the images on the website, it seems that most of the people that take the classes are older than that.
